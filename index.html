What is caMicroscope?

caMicroscope is a truly free scalable web-based biomedical image and data viewer, with strong emphasis for applications in cancer pathology. Try the demo

Using caMicroscope

caMicrosope aims to be easy-to-use. Nonetheless, the user-guide may be helpful in unlocking the power of our advanced scientific tools.

Developing caMicroscope

caMicrosope is open source. Any help, questions, and suggestions help not only you, but the entire caMicroscope community.
